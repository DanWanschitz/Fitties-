Welcome to
Fit Brain Lab!

The Fit Brain Lab focuses on developing exercise-based strategies to enhance stress resilience, particularly in students. Our research explores how physical exercise influences brain mechanisms that help protect against stress.

NEWS ACTIVITY TEAM

OUR GOAL

At The Fit Brain Lab, our goal is to bridge the gap between physical exercise and mental well-being by exploring how physical activity can increase stress resilience, particularly among students. We aim to develop and implement evidence-based strategies that enhance mental health through exercise, ensuring that students can thrive in both academic and personal aspects of their lives.

OUR MISSION

The mission of The Fit Brain Lab is to optimize existing physical exercise strategies and develop innovative approaches that specifically target stress resilience, with a focus on students. By enhancing the biological mechanisms in the brain that are influenced by exercise, we strive to provide effective tools for students to manage stress and improve their mental well-being.
