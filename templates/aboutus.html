The Fitbrain Lab

Exploring the Structural and Functional Plasticity of the Nervous System

Our Research Group

We are part of the research group 'Structural and Functional Plasticity of the Nervous System' and of the Swammerdam Institute for Life Sciences within the Faculty of Science of the University of Amsterdam.

At The Fitbrain Lab, we investigate how physical exercise affects brain function and structure, with implications for cognitive health, neuroplasticity, and neurological disorders.
